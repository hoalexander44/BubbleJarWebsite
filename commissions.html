Getting a Custom Plushie!

Email us at bubblejarart@gmail.com to order a custom plushie! Prices will be determined based on your request. Some factors that will be considered are materials and complexity.

Process

1. You send your inquiry through email
2. We agree on price and commission work
3. Full payment is required before we work on your order
4. We begin production on your order. We allow for 3 rounds of checkins where you can request for changes. (sketches, colors, render)
5. We email or mail you the final product
